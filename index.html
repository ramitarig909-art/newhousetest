About New House

Crafting
Beautiful Homes

"Every room deserves a story worth telling."

New House Furniture is a modern furniture showroom in Nasr City, Cairo, Egypt. We transform homes across Cairo into refined sanctuaries of calm and beauty — handcrafting bedrooms, living rooms, dining rooms, dressing-room wardrobes, TV units, shoe cabinets and coffee tables. Every piece is chosen with intention, and every room is designed with soul.

Materials & Quality
  • Frames, joints and battens in solid beech wood, with counter-grade surfaces and Auster polyester & Duco paint finishes.
  • Upholstery in velvet or reversible leather, with linen and other fabrics available to the customer's taste.
  • Sofas and corner units built on pure beech frames, 3cm thickness, with 33-density foam and a wide choice of fabrics.
  • Dressers and desks crafted from beech wood with counter surfaces and Auster polyester & Duco finishes.
  • Prices include glass, handles and fittings for every room, with free installation across Greater Cairo.
  • On-site measuring service for apartments and villas.
  • 5-year warranty against manufacturing defects, with flexible installment plans available.

More than furniture, we craft a home that reflects your lifestyle — with the comfort you deserve. Our vision is to be the first choice for furnishing apartments and villas in Egypt.
